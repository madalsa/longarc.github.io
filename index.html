Prescriptive Climate Solutions
for Long-Term Wellbeing

We build place-based, equitable, and affordable strategies for decarbonizing energy systems. Designed for today's constraints, our research shapes outcomes for generations.

Electricity Systems

Rate design, grid decarbonization, affordability, and integrating hyperscale demand onto electricity grids.

Sustainable Transport

Fleet electrification, vehicle retirement policies, and air pollution health impacts from light- and heavy-duty vehicles.

Energy Equity

Distributional impacts of energy policies, environmental justice, and ensuring clean energy access for underserved communities.
