Madalsa Singh

Principal Investigator · Assistant Professor

Madalsa studies carbon-constrained energy systems, with an emphasis on transport and electricity sectors. Her research develops place-based, equitable, and affordable strategies to rapidly reduce air emissions from energy use. Previously, she was a postdoctoral research fellow at UC Santa Barbara. She received her PhD from Stanford University and her undergraduate degree from IIT Bombay.
